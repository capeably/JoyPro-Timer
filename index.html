Welcome to JoyPro Timer
A focus timer with customizable sessions, beautiful themes, and sounds to keep you in flow.
▶
How It Works
Each session is made of segments that count down one by one. Press Space to start, and the timer handles the rest.
✎
Make It Yours
Double-click any title or time to edit it. Drag segments to reorder. Choose a theme and set per-segment sounds.
🌲
Watch It Grow
Try the Growing Tree theme: a bonsai tree grows as your timer runs, and the sky transitions from day to night.
Help & Tips
Keyboard Shortcuts
SpacePlay / Pause
NNext segment
MMute / Unmute
EEdit session
RReset session
EscClose dialogs
Editing
Double-click any segment title or the timer display to edit inline. Drag the handles in the segment list to reorder. Use the segment edit button (pencil icon) for full options including sound and theme.
Sessions
Create new sessions, save changes, or load saved ones. Export your sessions as JSON to back up or share, and import them on another device.
Themes
Light and Dark are clean, minimal themes. Starry Night features animated twinkling stars and shooting stars. Growing Tree grows a bonsai tree as your timer runs, with a full day-to-night sky cycle.
Sounds
Each segment can play a sound when it completes. Choose from 5 built-in sounds or upload your own (up to 500KB). Set per-segment sounds via the segment edit popover.
